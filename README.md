# Customer Orders Business Intelligence Analysis

A complete **end-to-end Data Analyst portfolio project** focused on extracting actionable business insights from customer order data using **SQL, Python, Excel, and Power BI**.

This project analyzes **50 customer order records** to evaluate sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product contribution, and monthly revenue trends.

## Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw order-level data into **decision-ready business intelligence** by calculating key KPIs, creating dimensional performance breakdowns, identifying top-performing products, and visualizing monthly trends.

## Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** → KPI extraction and aggregation logic
* **Python (Pandas)** → validation, grouping, and trend analysis
* **Excel** → pivot tables, formulas, and summary reporting
* **Power BI** → interactive dashboard and storytelling visuals

##  Dataset Overview

The dataset contains **50 customer order records** with the following fields:

* Customer_ID
* Customer_Name
* Order Date
* Segment
* Region
* Product_Category
* Product_Name
* Order Value
* Discount
* Profit

## Business KPIs Covered

* Total Revenue
* Total Net Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Average Order Value
* Revenue by Region
* Revenue by Product Category
* Revenue by Customer Segment
* Top 10 Products by Revenue
* Monthly Revenue & Profit Trend

## Analysis Performed

### 1) Overall Business Metrics

Calculated:

* Total Revenue
* Total Net Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Average Order Value

### 2) Performance Breakdown

Generated summary tables by:

* Geographic Region
* Product Category
* Customer Segment

### 3) Product Deep Dive

Identified and ranked:

* Top 10 products by total revenue
* corresponding total profit contribution

### 4) Time-Series Trend

Created:

* monthly revenue trend
* monthly profit trend
* month-wise performance fluctuation analysis

## Key Business Insights

This analysis helps identify:

* the most lucrative region, category, and segment
* top-performing products driving revenue
* monthly sales spikes and dips
* profitability trends across dimensions
* action areas for inventory and campaign optimization
